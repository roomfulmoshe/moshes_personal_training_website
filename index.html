MOSHE SHTAYGRUD ∕ PERSONAL TRAINER

I’m Moshe Shtaygrud, a dedicated personal trainer with a passion for helping everyday people move better, feel stronger, and live healthier. Over the years, I’ve guided clients of all ages and fitness levels — from complete beginners to competitive athletes — using training programs that are practical, safe, and results-driven. You bring the commitment; I’ll bring the expertise, support, and accountability to help you get there.
